I finished my dissertation in the direction of Elias M. Stein. Before that, I had a little experience in optimal control and dispersive equations, under supervision of Alberto Bressan and Athanassios S. Fokas.

My research is on several classical topics in harmonic analysis.

Selected papers and preprints

  • Classical solutions for differential inclusions with totally disconnected right hand side, joint with A. Bressan, J. Differential Equations, 2009.
  • On the controllability of Lagrangian system by active constraints, joint with A. Bressan, J. Differential Equations, 2009.
  • Generalized Dirichlet to Neumann maps for linear dispersive equations on the half-line, joint with A. S. Fokas, Mathematical Proceedings of the Cambridge Philosophical Society, 2018.
  • Θ-bump theorem for product fractional integrals, joint with E. Sawyer, Studia Mathematica, 2020.
  • Stein-Weiss inequality on product spaces, Revista Matemática Iberoamericana, 2021.
  • On the end-point of Stein-Weiss inequality, joint with C. Sun, The Journal of Geometric Analysis, 36:100 (2026).
  • L^p-boundedness of the Bochner-Riesz operator, Preprint 2025.
  • Multi-parameter fractional integration on Heisenberg group, joint with C. Sun, Preprint 2025.
  • H^1 to L^q-boundedness of fractional integral operators having a flag kernel, joint with J. Zhang, Preprint 2026.
